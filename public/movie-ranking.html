The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lorena, Light-Footed Woman

Not a movie, but a documentary. I was truly inspired by this very short documentary, and I often find myself thinking about her when I am tired. She is a girl who has lived through conditions that have callused her mind in a way that is just so crazy! If you ever get the chance, I highly recommend watching it. One of my favourite parts of the documentary is when they ask her if she wants Nike shoes, or something like that, and she giggles and says with a smirk on her face something like, "No thank you, because the people who wear those shoes always run behind me." She is an ultramarathon runner and wears sandals while doing it.


Soul

A great animation that reminded me about the simple pleasures of life.


The Alpinist

Features Marc-André Leclerc, a visionary Canadian climber who free soloed complex ice and alpine faces and ultimately died doing what he loved. Spoiler alert.
