About Me

I try not to do that anymore because I believe it limits me. Ironically though, haha, I now box myself in as a learner. I love to learn, to push my limits, and to become better in all aspects of my life.

What takes up most of my time as of late? Work for sure and I try to distribute the remainder of my time out into things that I love like, my soon to be partner, God, running, BJJ, coding, Mandarin, Spanish, family, nature, handstands, reading, making money, eating good food.. I guess its just overall improving. I need the friction. I need the growth

I love my life, love the present, at peace with the past and super damn excited about the future!
